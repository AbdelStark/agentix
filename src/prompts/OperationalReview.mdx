## Required Analysis

At minimum, explicitly inspect:
- Failure modes and retry/backoff/circuit-breaker behavior
- Rollout/rollback impact and blast radius
- Observability/logging/alerting implications
- Operational safeguards for degraded dependencies

Flag concrete issues with file-level specificity. Do not hand-wave.

## Severity Model

- `none`: no operational risk found.
- `low`: small readiness gap, non-blocking by default.
- `medium`: meaningful risk requiring remediation or explicit acceptance rationale.
- `high`: major readiness risk that blocks merge.
- `critical`: severe operational safety risk that blocks merge.

## Policy Rules

- `high` and `critical` always block.
- `medium` blocks unless fixed in review-fix or accepted with explicit rationale.
- If `approved: true` while severity is `medium`, `acceptanceRationale` must be non-empty.

## Output Contract

Return fields matching schema:
- `approved`
- `severity`
- `issues`: array of `{ severity, description, file, recommendation, check }`
- `remediationActions`: concrete fix actions
- `evidence`: concrete proof (runbooks, tests, risk controls, reasoning)
- `acceptanceRationale`: nullable string
